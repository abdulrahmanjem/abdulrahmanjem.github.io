Establishing a Vision

In December 2025, the **Kayan Relief Team** officially launched its operations. As the founder and manager, I established an organizational structure designed to streamline aid distribution and supervise large-scale humanitarian projects. The conference marked the beginning of a dedicated effort to serve thousands of beneficiaries in the Gaza Governorate.

Core Objectives

Volunteer Management

Overseeing dedicated teams to ensure efficient field operations.

Aid Distribution

Managing systematic logistics for thousands of displaced families.

Key Achievements Highlighted

During the conference, the team presented a roadmap for humanitarian intervention, focusing on:

  • Comprehensive operational plans for emergency relief.
  • Successful supervision of volunteer protocols.
  • Establishing partnerships for medical and engineering support.
