The Launch of Kayan Relief Team

A New Era of Humanitarian Engineering

In December 2025, the official launch conference for the Kayan Relief Team was held under the leadership of its founder, Abdulrahman Shunnar. The event marked the transition of a dedicated group of volunteers into a structured humanitarian organization focused on aid distribution and specialized relief operations.

The conference outlined the team's organizational structure, emphasizing the integration of engineering precision with humanitarian logistics to maximize the efficiency of aid reaching thousands of beneficiaries.

Core Objectives

  • • Structured Aid Distribution
  • • Volunteer Management Protocol
  • • Real-time Impact Reporting
  • • Engineering-led Logistics

Leading the Change

As the founder and manager, Abdulrahman Shunnar established a volunteer management protocol designed to supervise operations across multiple sectors. The team has already demonstrated its capability by managing aid for thousands of people in its first operational phase.
