Engineering Overview

Preparation of Electrosurgical Units (ESU) requires a rigorous understanding of high-frequency electrical safety. At **Yafa Hospital**, I supervised the calibration and clinical setup of these units to ensure precision in surgical cutting and coagulation, minimizing risks of thermal injury to patients.

Calibration

Adjusting power output levels for precise surgical response.

Patient Safety

Implementing return electrode monitoring (REM) protocols.

The Preparation Workflow
